My Story

After graduating from Trinity College in Hartford, CT in 2015 with a Bachelor's Degree in Mathematics and Art History, I spent the last five years working as a business systems analyst and engineer and as part of a team in various companies across the northeast US. I acquired project and time management skills, as well as the ability to communicate with team members and clients while effectively meeting milestones and deadlines.

As a systems engineer I have worked on a multitude of enterprise and web based applications for a range of clients providing analysis, design, development, testing and implementation. I have gained proficiency in a variety of platforms, languages and embedded systems.

I am currently working in Boston at Cognitec, the leading face recognition company in the world. When I'm not working I enjoy spending my free time biking around the city, painting and playing piano.
